Artificial intelligence aims to create machines capable of performing tasks that require human intelligence.
AI systems can analyze large datasets and identify patterns.
Machine learning is a core component of modern AI systems.
Deep learning uses neural networks with multiple layers.
Natural language processing enables machines to understand human language.
Computer vision allows machines to interpret visual information.
AI technologies are used in healthcare, finance, transportation, and robotics.
Autonomous vehicles rely on AI to navigate complex environments.
AI research continues to evolve rapidly with new algorithms and models.
Ethical considerations are important in the development of AI systems.
